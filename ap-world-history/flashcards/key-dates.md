# Key Dates Flashcards

Format: **Date** | Event | Significance

These are the most commonly tested dates on the AP exam. Memorize the approximate time period even if you forget the exact year.

---

## Unit 1-2: 1200-1450

**1206** | Genghis Khan unifies Mongols | Beginning of largest contiguous land empire

**1215** | Mongols capture Beijing | Expansion into China begins

**1258** | Mongols sack Baghdad | End of Abbasid Caliphate; major blow to Islamic world

**1271-1295** | Marco Polo's travels | Cultural exchange; sparked European interest in Asia

**1279** | Mongols conquer Song China | Yuan Dynasty established; Kublai Khan rules

**1324** | Mansa Musa's pilgrimage to Mecca | Demonstrated Mali's wealth; spread Islam

**1325-1354** | Ibn Battuta's travels | Documented Dar al-Islam across 75,000 miles

**1347-1351** | Black Death reaches Europe | Killed 30-60% of population; major social upheaval

**1368** | Ming Dynasty begins | Han Chinese restore rule after Mongol Yuan

**1405-1433** | Zheng He's voyages | Chinese maritime exploration (then stopped)

## Unit 3-4: 1450-1750

**1453** | Ottoman conquest of Constantinople | End of Byzantine Empire; Ottoman control of trade routes

**1492** | Columbus reaches Americas | Beginning of Columbian Exchange; transoceanic connections

**1494** | Treaty of Tordesillas | Spain and Portugal divide New World

**1498** | Vasco da Gama reaches India | Portuguese access to Indian Ocean trade

**1501** | Safavid Empire founded | Shia Islam becomes state religion in Persia

**1517** | Luther's 95 Theses | Beginning of Protestant Reformation

**1519-1521** | Cortés conquers Aztec Empire | Spain controls Mexico

**1526** | Battle of Panipat | Babur founds Mughal Empire in India

**1532-1533** | Pizarro conquers Inca Empire | Spain controls Peru

**1545** | Potosí silver discovered | Massive silver flows globally

**1556-1605** | Reign of Akbar the Great | Mughal golden age; religious tolerance

**1588** | Tokugawa Ieyasu unifies Japan | Beginning of Edo period and isolation

**1602** | Dutch East India Company founded | First multinational corporation

**1607** | Jamestown founded | First permanent English colony in Americas

**1619** | First Africans brought to Virginia | Beginning of slavery in British North America

**1644** | Qing Dynasty begins | Manchu conquest of Ming China

**1682-1725** | Reign of Peter the Great | Westernization of Russia

**1791** | Haitian Revolution begins | First successful slave revolution

**1850-1864** | Taiping Rebellion | Deadliest civil war (~20-30 million dead)

**1861** | Italian unification | Creation of united Italy

**1871** | German unification | Bismarck creates German Empire

## Unit 7: 1900-Present (Global Conflict)

**1914** | WWI begins | Assassination of Franz Ferdinand triggers war

**1915-1923** | Armenian Genocide | Ottoman Empire kills ~1.5 million Armenians

**1917** | Russian Revolution | Bolsheviks seize power; Lenin leads

**1918** | WWI ends | Treaty of Versailles (1919) punishes Germany

**1918-1919** | Spanish Flu pandemic | 50-100 million dead worldwide

**1929** | Great Depression begins | Stock market crash; global economic collapse

**1933** | Hitler becomes Chancellor | Nazi control of Germany

**1937** | Japan invades China | Beginning of WWII in Asia; Nanjing Massacre

**1939** | WWII begins in Europe | Germany invades Poland

**1941** | Pearl Harbor; U.S. enters WWII | Transforms war into truly global conflict

**1945** | WWII ends | Atomic bombs on Japan; Holocaust revealed

**1945** | United Nations founded | International organization for peace

## Memory Tips

### Chronological Anchors
- **1200** = Start of AP World course
- **1450** = Age of Exploration begins
- **1750** = Revolutions and Industrialization
- **1900** = Modern era (wars, Cold War, globalization)

### Pattern Recognition
- **47s are important**: 1347 (Black Death), 1947 (Truman Doctrine, India independence)
- **89s mean change**: 1789 (French Revolution), 1989 (Berlin Wall falls)
- **45s end wars**: 1945 (WWII), 1945 (UN founded)
